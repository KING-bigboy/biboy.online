About Evolution Factor

Evolution Factor is a captivating survival and evolution game where you begin your journey as a humble organism in a vast, competitive world. The core objective is simple yet addictive: consume smaller life forms to gain mass, grow larger, and evolve into a more powerful and complex creature.

The game world is a dangerous place, teeming with other players all vying for supremacy. You must strategically navigate the environment, hunting prey while deftly avoiding larger predators. Each evolutionary stage unlocks new abilities and appearances, making every playthrough a unique journey of survival and growth.

How to Play Evolution Factor

Movement: Use your Mouse to guide your creature. It will automatically move towards your cursor on the screen.

Survival Strategy: Your main goal is to eat smaller creatures (often highlighted in green) to gain experience and grow. You must avoid larger creatures (often highlighted in red), as they will consume you!

Evolution: As you consume enough food, an evolution bar will fill up. Once full, you will automatically transform into the next, more powerful life form, allowing you to prey on a wider variety of creatures.

Goal: Survive as long as possible, evolve through all the stages, and become the most dominant creature in the ecosystem. Aim for the highest score!
